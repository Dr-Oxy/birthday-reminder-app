Are you hopeless at remembering birthdays?

Here’s an app that lets you save contact’s birthday, name, phone number and more importantly creates a reminder for you one hour before so you don’t forget. How’s this different from the event calendar on your phone? Well, Birthday reminder app also let’s you create messages ready to be sent on the birthday date.

How it works

Sign Up

Sign up in two easy steps: enter your email address, phone number and press sign up.

Create Reminder

Start creating birthday reminders by clicking on the plus button on the welcome home screen

Choose Contact

Allow Birthday reminder to access your contact storage and select contact's name and phone number

Set Up Reminder

Select birth date, reminder's time and choose either the customise or random message preference category

Customise Messages

In this message preference option, you can create your own unique birthday wishes. Hit save. All done.

Random Messages

Birthday Reminder crafts messages according to the labels. Labels include: siblings, colleagues, besties etc.
